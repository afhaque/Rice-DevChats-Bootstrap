About DevChats

What's all the commotion?

Rice DevChats is a student-led workshop series for Rice programmers, developers, designers, and all-around creative enthusiasts. The motivation is simple. Learning new things is sometimes hard. But learning new things on your own is always harder.

Everyone knows that technology is always changing. And whether you're an all-star coding prodigy or a slacker who feels chronically left behind, it's always helpful when someone hands you the cliffnotes. Our idea is to hand you the guy (or girl) who has the cliffnotes.
